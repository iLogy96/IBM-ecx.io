Frontend bootcamp started

We are happy to announce that the Frontend Bootcamp just started with 10 interns from different locations and with different backgrounds. Their next four months are all about HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

A warm welcome to our new developers!

The bootcamp has successfully started yesterday. In total the paid internship will run for 4 months. During the bootcamp our frontend new joiners will be coached by four trainers and 4 assistants.

During this time they will learn everything they need to know about HTML, CSS and JavaScript (and Frontend related topics). The concept of the bootcamp includes an intensive training from Monday to Thursday and a weekly recap and task solving on Fridays.
